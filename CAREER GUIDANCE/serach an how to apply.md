### How To Find Out Fast

**Just apply and be upfront in your cover note:**

> _"I'm based in India, available for fully remote work, able to work flexible hours to overlap with UK timezone. Happy to work as freelance contractor invoicing directly."_

Worst case they say no. Best case they don't care.

- **"DevOps remote worldwide"**
- **"DevOps remote global"**
- **"DevOps contractor remote timezone flexible"**

Or filter by:

- Location: **Worldwide** or **Anywhere**

### Honest Reality

UK/US "remote" jobs are harder but not impossible from India. Your best targets are:

- Startups with no HR department
- Companies that already hire globally
- Upwork/Toptal where location truly doesn't matter

Don't get discouraged — apply anyway. Some will say yes.
