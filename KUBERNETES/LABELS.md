In Kubernetes, **labels are key-value pairs attached to resources**, and **selectors use those labels to find resources**. A ReplicaSet selector matches Pod labels to identify which Pods it manages and maintains the required replica count, while a Service selector matches Pod labels to identify which Pods should receive traffic. **All labels specified in the selector must match the Pod**, but the Pod can have additional labels. The key idea is: **ReplicaSet selector → Pod labels for management; Service selector → Pod labels for traffic routing.**
