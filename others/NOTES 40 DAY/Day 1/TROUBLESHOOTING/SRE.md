Here are 10 common microservices / distributed system failure scenarios SREs frequently debug:
- **Service-to-service network connectivity failure**
    
- **DNS/service discovery resolution failure**
    
- **Database connection pool exhaustion**
    
- **Message queue backlog or consumer lag (RabbitMQ/Kafka)**
    
- **Cache failure or cache stampede (Redis)**
    
- **Configuration drift or wrong environment variables**
    
- **Resource exhaustion (CPU, memory, disk, file descriptors)**
    
- **API latency cascading across dependent services**
    
- **Authentication/authorization token failures**
    
- **Load balancer or health check misconfiguration**
