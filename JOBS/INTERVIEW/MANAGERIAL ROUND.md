For a **Senior DevOps Engineer (9+ years overall, 4+ years in DevOps)**, managerial rounds are usually less about commands and more about how you think, communicate, prioritize, and handle production situations.

Here are the questions you're most likely to face.

### About You

- Walk me through your career.
- Why are you looking for a change?
- Why do you want to join our company?
- What has been your biggest achievement in your current role?
- Tell me about a project you're most proud of.
- Describe a challenging production incident you handled.

### Ownership & Decision Making

- Tell me about a time you disagreed with a developer. How did you resolve it?
- Have you ever pushed back on a request? Why?
- How do you prioritize multiple production issues?
- Tell me about a mistake you made and what you learned.
- When do you automate, and when do you avoid automation?

### Leadership

- Have you mentored junior engineers?
- How do you conduct code reviews for Terraform or Kubernetes manifests?
- If a junior engineer keeps making the same mistake, what would you do?
- How do you share knowledge within your team?

### Production Scenarios

- Your application is down. What is your first action?
- Deployment failed halfway. What would you do?
- CPU utilization suddenly reaches 100%. How do you investigate?
- Kubernetes pods are CrashLoopBackOff. Walk me through your troubleshooting.
- Terraform apply failed after creating half the infrastructure. What's your approach?
- Jenkins pipeline has been failing since yesterday. How do you investigate?

### Communication

- Explain Kubernetes to a non-technical manager.
- Explain Terraform to a new team member.
- How do you communicate during a production outage?
- If management asks why an outage happened, what do you say?

### DevOps Culture

- What does DevOps mean to you?
- Is DevOps a role or a culture?
- How do developers and operations work together?
- How do you improve deployment frequency while reducing risk?

### Security

- How do you secure secrets?
- How do you handle IAM permissions?
- What security practices do you include in CI/CD?
- Have you worked with vulnerability scanning?

### Behavioral Questions (STAR Method)

- Tell me about a conflict.
- Tell me about a failure.
- Tell me about a time you worked under pressure.
- Tell me about a time you had to learn something quickly.
- Tell me about a time you improved a process.
- Tell me about a time you took ownership beyond your responsibilities.

### Team Fit

- What type of manager brings out your best work?
- How do you handle criticism?
- What motivates you?
- What frustrates you at work?
- How do you deal with difficult teammates?

### Questions Specific to DevOps

- Describe your CI/CD pipeline end to end.
- Explain your Terraform module strategy.
- How do you manage Terraform state?
- How do you perform Kubernetes upgrades?
- What monitoring stack have you implemented?
- How do you reduce cloud costs?
- Describe your disaster recovery strategy.

### Questions You Should Ask the Manager

These leave a strong impression:

- What does success look like in the first 90 days?
- What are the biggest challenges the DevOps team is facing today?
- How mature is your Kubernetes platform?
- How is infrastructure managed today?
- How do developers and DevOps teams collaborate?
- What are the major initiatives planned over the next year?
- How do you measure the success of this role?
